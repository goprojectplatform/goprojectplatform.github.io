Project Platform.

Our Mission.

Project Platform was created for one reason only.
To give people a platform on the internet.
We believe that plain resumes can be transformed into beautiful sites which represent more than a piece of paper can.

Our Services.

At Project Platform, we offer two services. A single page website, or a multi page website, based off how detailed you'd prefer your website to be. All of our services are completely free-of-charge, and we do not accept any payment. However, there may be a wait to get a service in a case with a high volume of requests.
